Most Important Organizations


UFC

Sport: Mixed Martial Arts

Founded: 1993

The UFC is the largest and most recognized MMA promotion in the world. Known for its deep roster of elite fighters, the UFC has popularized MMA and made stars out of athletes like Conor McGregor and Khabib Nurmagomedov. It hosts events globally and has multiple weight divisions.


WBA

Sport: Boxing

Founded: 1962

The WBA is one of the four major sanctioning bodies in professional boxing. It was originally called the National Boxing Association but changed to the WBA in the 1960s. The organization ranks fighters and awards titles across multiple weight divisions.


ONE Championship

Sport: MMA, Muay Thai, Kickboxing

Founded: 2011

Based in Asia, ONE Championship features MMA, Muay Thai, and kickboxing. The organization emphasizes martial arts values and has a wide-reaching fan base across Asia. ONE has a unique focus on lighter weight classes and martial arts traditions.


ADCC

Sport: BJJ

Founded: 1998

The ADCC organizes the most prestigious submission grappling event in the world, the ADCC World Championship. It uses a specific rule set that emphasizes submissions and allows for wrestling and other grappling styles.
